Three Destinations You Must Visit in Indonesia

Lake Toba

Lake Toba is an extraordinary natural wonder of the world. This enermous crater lake consists of an island almost the size of Singapore in its center. At over 1,145 square km, and a depth of 450 meter, Lake Toba is actually more like an ocean. This is the largest lake in Southeast Asia and one of the deepest lakes in the world.

    Attractions

  • Toba lake has a beautiful pristine scenery and picturesque mountains set against the cool clear lake.
  • The climate there is cooler which gives a well-needed break from the heat, humidity, and pollution of the city.
  • Kayaking in Lake Toba.
  • See wooden puppet called Sigale-gale.

Labuan Bajo

On the westernmost tip of the island of Flores sits the town of Labuan Bajo. It lies peacefully, with many wonders waiting for you to explore. Labuan Bajo was onlu a small fishing site, but flourished to become the gateaway to many exotic destinations in East Nusa Tenggara.

    Attractions

  • The extraordinary Komodo National Park.
  • The city of sunset. You can enjoy some spectacular views of the island and the open sea at sunset.
  • Rangko Cave.
  • Padar Island, an undeniable travel wish list.
  • Pink beach.

Raja Ampat

Far from the view-blocking skyscrapers, dense and hectic concrete jungles, congested traffics, flickering electric billboards, endless annoying noises, and all the nuisances of modern cities, you will find a pristine paradise where Mother Nature and warm fiendly people welcome you with all the exceptional wonders in Raja Ampat, the island-regency in West Papua Province. With all the spectacular wonders above and beyond its waters, as well as on land and amidst the thick jungles, thsi is truly the place where words such as beautiful, enchanting, magnificent, and fascinating get its true physical meaning.

    Attractions

  • Wonderful Waigeo Island.
  • Sawinggrai Village.
  • Misool Island.
  • Kofiau Island.
  • Wayag Island.
